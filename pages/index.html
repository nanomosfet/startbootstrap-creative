Late Pick-up/arrival:

Parents are responsible to drop off and pickup their child by the time stated on their enrollment agreement. Any changes in your regular routine and if you will be arriving early or picking up late I must be notified of these changes ahead of time. I can make arrangements to care for your child until 5:00pm. However, if you will be later then 6:00pm, it is required that you pay and overtime fee of 1 for every minute passed 6:00pm. Under no circumstances will this policy be waived. Please be courteous and be on time.

Our Mission

Daisy's Daycare mission is to provide a safe, developmentally appropriate, at home, environment for every child. Our focus as a childcare provider is to provide a stimulating educational experience which promotes each child's social/emotional, physical and cognitive development. Our goal is support children's desire to be life-long learners. We will guide each child to develop skills necessary to learn, live together, think divergently and be unified.
